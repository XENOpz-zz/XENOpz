DM_Frost

This level depicts a forsaken science facility on a frozen planet, just barely renovated by the Liandri corporation for the sake using it as a field for the Unreal Tournament. The colours are generally cool and a variety of cold and "windy" visual effects are scattered across the level to deepen the atmosphere of sub-zero temperatures.

A central room holds a mega health bonus, but is very exposed. Furthermore, a custom script makes everyone walking over the ice have a chance to slip and fall down for a short time. A hole in the wall allows for wind and snow to enter the complex.

A room in one of the corners of the science complex has server-like lights, while the room next to it is being flooded by a damaging goo to reflect the state of the building. A bonus is hidden at the top of the room.
