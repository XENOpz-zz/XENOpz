Escape the Rising Lava

About the Game:

This is a Unity game made for about 3 hours. It features two freely available sound effects, but other than that everything else was made by me. The goal of the game is to escape the rising lava (get to the blue block), for which one has 10 seconds. You can download and play the game here. Controls are standard: W, A, S, D for movement, Spacebar for jumping, Mouse to look around.
