WAR_TwoWorlds

This map is made for the Warfare gamemode, where each team has a base core. Players strive to control power nodes along the map, which allow them to damage the enemies' core. Opposed to taking a symmetrical approach, this map gives one team an underwater base, while the other team's core is positioned high on a mountain.

The two bases are connected through power nodes in aerial passageways in the ocean, a big field and the inside of the mountain that hosts one of the teams' cores. Inside the mountain there is a complex system of walkways and jump-boosters that allow attackers to reach the top of the mountain.

The underwater team's base is protected by narrow passageways and obviously water. Its interior is robot-factory-themed.
