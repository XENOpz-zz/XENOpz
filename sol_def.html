Sol Defender

About the Game:

This game was done as an overkill project for a university homework for the first semester module about Flash. I have cooperated with a coursemate to create a game, that integrates three mini-games and finally a boss fight. While the mouse-avoider levels and the level-selection screen have been made by my teammate, I have created the platformer and space shooter mini-games, as well as the final boss fight. I am also proud to say I have drawn all of the sprites in my levels (except for the background planets, which I edited from pictures from google).

How to Play:

The big red button on top of the level selection screen is a cheat button. It unlocks all levels (including the sun boss).

Please read the instructions in-game for more details on controls.
