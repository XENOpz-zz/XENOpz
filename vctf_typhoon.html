vCTF_Typhoon

This vehicle-capture-the-flag map is named after its setting: the eye of a raging space-storm. The two teams try to cross the map either on foot or in a flying vehicle to get the other team's flag to their base. A deadly vertical drop and a narrow bridge make the fight very dynamic, while still allowing more stealthy players to cross either by using the floating platforms or the outside ledges of the bridge.

Both bases are mirrored. They both have two floors, the second one housing the flag. On the roof there is a landing pad for a raptor (flying vehicle). The second floor has an automatic door that allows a flag carrier to quickly escape the enemy's base.

The map also provides the players with eye-candy: a star and an asteroid belt are visible in the background.
