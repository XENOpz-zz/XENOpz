Project SSG

About the Game:

This prototype is based on oldschool space shooter games. That is what the SSG in the name stands for: Space Shooter Game. It is done in Flash during my first year of University as a personal project. A coursemate has helped me with drawing the spaceship sprites. I have experimented with a lot of ideas in this game, including a simple built-in music player, moving ship parts (the guns rotate to aim at the mouse), composite enemies. The spaceships' sprites are all made by a colleague of mine from Kingston University in London and the sound effects and music are from free music/sound effects websites (except for the intro and credits songs). Everything else has been done by me. The prototype includes a fully working level, a demonstration upgrade screen and a placeholder (empty) second level.

How to Play:

Don't get hit! Regenerating your shields uses up power, which you need for shooting!

W,A,S,D to move the ship.

Spacebar to skip intro and shoot main weapon straight.

LMB to shoot alt weapons at cursor.

Shift to release ultimate weapon (only works when it is cooled down and enough energy is present).

Escape to skip overly long intro.
